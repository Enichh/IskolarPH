## Getting Started

### System Requirements

- **Device**: Android smartphone or tablet
- **Operating System**: Android 7.0 (API 24) or higher
- **Storage**: At least 50MB free space
- **Internet**: Required for authentication and AI features
- **GPS**: Optional, for location-based recommendations

### Installing the App

#### Method 1: Download APK (Recommended)

1. Visit [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/Enichh/ISKOLARPH/releases/latest)
2. Download `ISKOLARPH-v1.0-release.apk`
3. Open the downloaded file
4. If prompted, allow installation from unknown sources:
   - Go to **Settings** > **Security**
   - Enable **"Unknown Sources"** or **"Install Unknown Apps"**
   - Select your browser and allow
5. Tap **Install**
6. Open ISKOLARPH

#### Method 2: Build from Source

For developers or advanced users, see [README.md](../README.md#installation).

## Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Registration

1. Open ISKOLARPH app
2. Tap **"Sign Up"** on the login screen
3. Fill in your information:

| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Email** | Valid email address | juan.delacruz@email.com |
| **Password** | At least 6 characters | ******** |
| **First Name** | Your given name | Juan |
| **Last Name** | Your surname | Dela Cruz |
| **Middle Initial** | Optional | M |
| **GWA** | Your Grade Weighted Average (1.00-5.00) | 1.75 |
| **Location** | Your region | Manila, Luzon |

4. Tap **"Sign Up"** to create your account

### Step 2: Email Verification

⚠️ **Important**: You must verify your email before using the app.

1. Check your email inbox (and spam folder)
2. Look for an email from ISKOLARPH
3. Click the **verification link**
4. Return to the app
5. Tap **"I've verified my email"**

**Didn't receive the email?**
- Wait 60 seconds and tap **"Resend"**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Ensure you entered the correct email address

### Step 3: First Login

1. Enter your registered email
2. Enter your password
3. Tap **"Login"**
4. You'll be taken to the Dashboard

## Finding Scholarships

### Browsing the Catalog

1. Tap **Catalog** in the bottom navigation
2. Browse the list of available scholarships
3. Each card shows:
   - **Scholarship Name**
   - **Provider Organization**
   - **Location** (Luzon, Visayas, Mindanao, National)
   - **Award Amount** (in PHP)
   - **Application Deadline**

### Using Search

1. Tap the **search bar** at the top
2. Type keywords:
   - Scholarship name (e.g., "CHED")
   - Provider (e.g., "SM Foundation")
   - Field of study (e.g., "Engineering")
3. Results update automatically as you type

### Applying Filters

#### Location Filter
1. Tap the **location icon** (📍) next to the search bar
2. Select a region:
   - **All** - Show all scholarships
   - **Luzon** - Luzon-only scholarships
   - **Visayas** - Visayas-only scholarships
   - **Mindanao** - Mindanao-only scholarships
   - **National** - Nationwide scholarships

#### GPA/GWA Filter
1. Tap the **filter icon** (⚡) next to the search bar
2. Toggle **"Show scholarships I qualify for"**
3. Only scholarships matching your GWA will appear

**Note**: You must complete your profile with a valid GWA for this to work.

### Viewing Scholarship Details

1. Tap any scholarship card
2. View complete information:
   - **Description** - What the scholarship offers
   - **Provider** - Organization offering the scholarship
   - **Award Amount** - Financial support amount
   - **Location** - Eligible regions
   - **Deadline** - Application closing date
   - **Eligibility** - Requirements you must meet
   - **Application Link** - Direct link to apply

3. Tap **"Click here to apply"** to visit the application website

### Saving Scholarships

1. Tap the **bookmark icon** (🔖) on any scholarship card
2. The icon fills to indicate it's saved
3. View saved scholarships in the **Saved** tab
4. Tap again to unsave

## Using the AI Assistant

ISKOLARPH includes a built-in AI chatbot to help with scholarship-related questions.

### Opening the Chatbot

1. Tap the **floating chat button** (💬) on any screen
   - Located in the bottom-right corner
   - Available on Home, Catalog, and Saved screens

2. The chat dialog will open

### What You Can Ask

The AI assistant can help with:

✅ **Scholarship Information**
- "What scholarships are available for engineering students?"
- "Tell me about the CHED scholarship"
- "What are the requirements for DOST scholarship?"

✅ **Application Tips**
- "How do I write a good scholarship essay?"
- "What should I include in my application letter?"
- "Tips for scholarship interviews"

✅ **Eligibility Questions**
- "What GWA do I need for academic scholarships?"
- "Can I apply for multiple scholarships?"
- "Do I qualify if I'm a working student?"

✅ **Study Advice**
- "How can I improve my grades?"
- "Time management tips for students"
- "How to prepare for scholarship exams"

❌ **Off-topic questions** will be politely redirected to educational topics.

### Chat Features

- **Message History**: Your current conversation is maintained
- **Message Limit**: Maximum 20 messages per session
- **Auto-reset**: Conversation resets automatically after reaching limit
- **Language**: Supports English and Filipino

### Example Conversation

```
You: Hi! What scholarships are good for IT students?

AI: Hello! Here are some great scholarships for IT students:

1. **DOST-SEI Scholarship** - For STEM students including IT
   - Full tuition + stipend
   - Requires 85% GWA or higher

2. **SM College Scholarship** - Open to all courses
   - Full tuition + allowance
   - Grade requirement varies

3. **Megaworld Foundation** - For IT/Business students
   - Tuition coverage
   - Interview required

Would you like details on any specific scholarship?
```

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "Email not verified" error

**Problem**: You can't log in because your email isn't verified.

**Solution**:
1. Check your email inbox and spam folder
2. Click the verification link
3. If link expired, tap "Resend" on the verification screen
4. Wait 60 seconds between resend attempts

#### "Invalid credentials" error

**Problem**: Login fails with email/password error.

**Solution**:
1. Check Caps Lock is off
2. Verify you're using the registered email
3. Try resetting your password:
   - Tap "Forgot Password?" on login
   - Enter your email
   - Check email for reset link
   - Create new password

#### Location not working

**Problem**: Can't detect your location for recommendations.

**Solution**:
1. Go to phone **Settings** > **Apps** > **ISKOLARPH**
2. Tap **Permissions**
3. Enable **Location**
4. Set to **"Allow all the time"** or **"Allow while using"**
5. Restart the app

#### No scholarships showing

**Problem**: Catalog appears empty.

**Solution**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Clear app data:
   - **Settings** > **Apps** > **ISKOLARPH**
   - Tap **Storage** > **Clear Cache**
3. Restart the app
4. If still empty, contact support

#### App crashes on startup

**Problem**: App closes immediately when opened.

**Solution**:
1. Force stop the app:
   - **Settings** > **Apps** > **ISKOLARPH**
   - Tap **Force Stop**
2. Clear app cache:
   - **Settings** > **Apps** > **ISKOLARPH**
   - Tap **Storage** > **Clear Cache**
3. Restart your phone
4. Reinstall if problem persists

### Error Messages Reference

| Error Message | Meaning | Solution |
|---------------|---------|----------|
| "Authentication failed" | Wrong email/password | Check credentials, reset password if needed |
| "Email already in use" | Account exists | Use "Forgot Password" or login instead |
| "Weak password" | Password too short | Use at least 6 characters |
| "Invalid email" | Email format wrong | Check for typos, use valid format |
| "Network error" | No internet | Check WiFi/mobile data connection |
| "Session expired" | Login timed out | Login again |

## Privacy & Security

### Data We Collect

ISKOLARPH collects minimal data to provide scholarship matching:

| Data | Purpose | Storage |
|------|---------|---------|
| Email | Authentication | Firebase (encrypted) |
| Name | Personalization | Firebase + Local DB |
| GWA | Eligibility matching | Local DB only |
| Location | Regional scholarships | Local DB only |

### Data We Don't Collect

- ❌ Passwords (handled securely by Firebase)
- ❌ Browsing history outside app
- ❌ Personal documents
- ❌ Application submissions

### Security Features

✅ **Email Verification** - Prevents fake accounts  
✅ **Secure Authentication** - Firebase Auth with encryption  
✅ **Local Data Storage** - Sensitive data stays on your device  
✅ **No Third-party Sharing** - Your data isn't sold or shared  

### Your Rights

- **Access**: View all your stored data in Profile
- **Correction**: Update information anytime
- **Deletion**: Request account deletion in Privacy Settings
- **Export**: Contact support for data export

### Contact for Privacy Concerns

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests:
- Open a GitHub issue: [github.com/Enichh/ISKOLARPH/issues](https://github.com/Enichh/ISKOLARPH/issues)

## FAQ

**Q: Is ISKOLARPH free to use?**  
A: Yes, completely free for all Filipino students.

**Q: Do you process scholarship applications?**  
A: No, we only help you find scholarships. You apply directly through official channels.

**Q: Can I use ISKOLARPH without internet?**  
A: Partially. Browse saved scholarships offline, but need internet for search and AI features.

**Q: How often is scholarship data updated?**  
A: We regularly update our database with new opportunities.

**Q: Can international students use ISKOLARPH?**  
A: The app is designed for Filipino students, but some scholarships may accept international applicants.

**Q: What if I find incorrect scholarship information?**  
A: Please report it via GitHub issues so we can verify and update.
